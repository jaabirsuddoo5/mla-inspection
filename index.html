Professional Snagging & Defect Management

Getting Started

  • Create a project — Name and Ref No. are required
  • Open the project and go to Inspection tab to add items
  • Each item needs a Category before saving

Inspection Items

  • Tap + Add to create a snag item
  • Capture Before and After photos from your camera
  • Annotate photos with arrows, circles, and text
  • Swipe right → Closed | left → In Progress
  • Group by Location, Category, Contractor, or Priority

Floor Plans

  • Go to Plans tab and tap + to add a plan
  • Upload an image (max 5MB)
  • Enable Pin Mode to drop pins on the plan
  • Tap a pin to see item info or remove it

Cover & Export

  • Go to Cover tab — upload wallpaper, logo, fill project info
  • Go to Export tab and tap Download Word Report
  • Open in Word to tweak formatting
